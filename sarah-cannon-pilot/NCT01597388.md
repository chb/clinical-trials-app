Target Profile
```
Patient's age must be greater than or equal to 18 years.
Patient's gender must be female.
Patient must have active diagnosis of Breast cancer.
Patient must have active diagnosis of Estrogen receptor positive tumor.
Patient must have active diagnosis of Metastasis from malignant tumor of breast.
Patient must have a health evaluation score of ECOG performance status grade:Prid:Pt:^Patient:Nom:Observed less than or equal to 1.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Type 1 diabetes mellitus.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Type II diabetes mellitus uncontrolled.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Ventricular arrhythmia.
Patient must not have historical medication within drug class  ANDROGENS/ANABOLICS.
Patient must not have historical medication with ingredient Thalidomide.
Patient must not have procedure of Immunotherapy.
```
