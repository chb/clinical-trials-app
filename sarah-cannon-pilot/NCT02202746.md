Target Profile
```
Patient's age must be greater than or equal to 18 years.
Patient must have active diagnosis of Breast cancer.
Patient must have active diagnosis of Metastasis from malignant tumor of breast.
Patient must have mutation of FGFR1 amplification OR 11q (FGF3, FGF4, Cyclin D1, or FGF19) amplification.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of CNS metastases.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Heart disease.
Patient must not be pregnant.
Patient must not be breastfeeding.
```
