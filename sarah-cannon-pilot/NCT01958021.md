Target Profile
```
Patient's gender must be female.
Patient must have active diagnosis of Breast cancer.
Patient must have active diagnosis of Estrogen receptor positive tumor OR Progesterone receptor positive tumor.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of HER2-positive carcinoma of breast.
Patient must have a health evaluation score of ECOG performance status grade:Prid:Pt:^Patient:Nom:Observed less than or equal to 1.
Patient must not have historical medication within drug class CDK 4/6 inhibitor.
Patient must not have active medication within drug class  ANTINEOPLASTICS.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Angina pectoris within less than or equal to 12 months.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Heart attack within less than or equal to 12 months.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Congestive heart failure AND New York Heart Association Classification - Class III.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Congestive heart failure AND New York Heart Association Classification - Class IV.
Patient must not have most recent measurement of Left ventricular Ejection fraction less than 50 %.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Ventricular arrhythmia within less than or equal to 12 months.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Supraventricular arrhythmia within less than or equal to 12 months.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Nodal arrhythmia within less than or equal to 12 months.
```
