Target Profile
```
Patient's age must be greater than or equal to 18 years.
Patient's gender must be female.
Patient must have active diagnosis of Breast cancer.
Patient must have active diagnosis of Estrogen receptor positive tumor.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of CNS metastases.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Metastasis to brain.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Acute pancreatitis.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Chronic pancreatitis.
Patient must not have most recent lab value of Hgb A1c MFr Bld greater than 6.5 %.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Gestational diabetes mellitus.
```
