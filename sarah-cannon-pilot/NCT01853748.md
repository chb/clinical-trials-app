Target Profile
```
Patient's age must be greater than or equal to 18 years.
Patient must have active diagnosis of HER2-positive carcinoma of breast.
Patient must have cancer stage of Clinical stage I.
Patient must not have procedure of Breast surgery within greater than 90 days.
Patient must not have historical medication within drug class  INVESTIGATIONAL AGENTS within less than or equal to 2 weeks.
Patient must not be pregnant.
Patient must not be breastfeeding.
Patient must not have historical medication with ingredient trastuzumab.
Patient must not have historical medication with ingredient Paclitaxel.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Liver disease.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Congestive heart failure.
```
