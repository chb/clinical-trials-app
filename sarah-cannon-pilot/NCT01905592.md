Target Profile
```
Patient's age must be greater than or equal to 18 years.
Patient must have active diagnosis of Breast cancer.
Patient must have historical medication within drug class Anthracycline Topoisomerase Inhibitor OR with ingredient Paclitaxel OR with ingredient docetaxel OR with ingredient ixabepilone.
Patient must have a health evaluation score of ECOG performance status grade:Prid:Pt:^Patient:Nom:Observed less than or equal to 2.
Patient must not be pregnant.
Patient must not be breastfeeding.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Hepatitis B.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Viral hepatitis C.
Patient must not have historical medication with mechanism of action PARP inhibitor.
```
