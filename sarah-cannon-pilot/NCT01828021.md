Target Profile
```
Patient's age must be greater than or equal to 18 years.
Patient must have active diagnosis of Breast cancer.
Patient must have active diagnosis of Metastasis from malignant tumor of breast OR Local recurrence of malignant tumor of breast.
Patient must have a health evaluation score of ECOG performance status grade:Prid:Pt:^Patient:Nom:Observed less than or equal to 1.
Patient must have most recent measurement of Left ventricular Ejection fraction greater than or equal to 50 %.
Patient must not have historical medication within drug class monoclonal antibodies within less than or equal to 28 days.
Patient must not have allergy to substance polysorbate 80.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Deep vein thrombosis within less than or equal to 3 months.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Pulmonary embolism within less than or equal to 3 months.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Stroke within less than or equal to 3 months.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Heart attack.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Congestive heart failure AND New York Heart Association Classification - Class III.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Congestive heart failure AND New York Heart Association Classification - Class IV.
```
