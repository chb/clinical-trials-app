Target Profile
```
Patient's age must be greater than or equal to 18 years.
Patient must have active diagnosis of Human epidermal growth factor 2 negative carcinoma of breast.
Patient must have active diagnosis of Metastasis from malignant tumor of breast OR Local recurrence of malignant tumor of breast.
Patient must have a health evaluation score of ECOG performance status grade:Prid:Pt:^Patient:Nom:Observed less than or equal to 1.
IF patient's gender must be female, THEN patient must agree to use an acceptable and effective method of non-hormonal contraception.
Patient must not have procedure of Radiation therapy care within less than or equal to 3 weeks.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of HER2-positive carcinoma of breast.
Patient must not have historical medication within drug class  ANTINEOPLASTIC HORMONES within less than or equal to 2 weeks.
Patient must not have historical medication within drug class  ANTINEOPLASTIC ADJUVANTS within less than or equal to 12 months.
Patient must not have historical medication within drug class  INVESTIGATIONAL AGENTS within less than or equal to 28 days.
Patient must not be pregnant.
Patient must not be breastfeeding.
```
