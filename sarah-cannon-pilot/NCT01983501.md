Target Profile
```
Patient's age must be greater than or equal to 18 years.
Patient must have active diagnosis of HER2-positive carcinoma of breast.
Patient must have active diagnosis of Metastasis from malignant tumor of breast.
Patient must not be pregnant.
Patient must agree to use two concurrent forms of contraception.
Patient must have a health evaluation score of ECOG performance status grade:Prid:Pt:^Patient:Nom:Observed less than or equal to 1.
Patient must have most recent lab value of Hemoglobin [Mass/volume] in Blood greater than or equal to 9 g/dL.
Patient must have most recent lab value of Platelets [#/volume] in Blood greater than or equal to 100 10*3/uL.
Patient must have most recent lab value of Creatinine 24H renal clearance panel greater than or equal to 60 mL/min.
Patient must not be breastfeeding.
Patient must not have historical medication with ingredient trastuzumab within less than or equal to 3 weeks.
Patient must not have historical medication within drug class  ANTINEOPLASTIC HORMONES within less than or equal to 2 weeks.
Patient must not have historical medication with ingredient lapatinib within less than or equal to 4 weeks.
Patient must not have historical medication with ingredient neratinib within less than or equal to 4 weeks.
Patient must not have historical medication with ingredient afatinib within less than or equal to 4 weeks.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Leptomeningeal sarcoma.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of HIV infection.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Hepatitis B.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Viral hepatitis C.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Liver disease.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Autoimmune hepatitis.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Sclerosing cholangitis.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Congestive heart failure.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Heart attack within less than or equal to 6 months.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Unstable angina within less than or equal to 6 months.
```
