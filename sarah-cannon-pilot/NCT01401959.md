Target Profile
```
Patient's age must be greater than or equal to 18 years.
Patient's gender must be female.
Patient must have active diagnosis of Breast cancer.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Metastasis from malignant tumor of breast.
Patient must have historical medication with ingredient docetaxel OR with ingredient Paclitaxel OR within drug class Anthracycline Topoisomerase Inhibitor.
IF patient must have active diagnosis of HER2-positive carcinoma of breast, THEN patient must have historical medication with ingredient trastuzumab.
Patient must have a health evaluation score of ECOG performance status grade:Prid:Pt:^Patient:Nom:Observed less than or equal to 1.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Renal insufficiency.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Hepatic insufficiency.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of CNS metastases.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Heart attack within less than or equal to 6 months.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Congestive heart failure AND New York Heart Association Classification - Class II.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Congestive heart failure AND New York Heart Association Classification - Class III.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Congestive heart failure AND New York Heart Association Classification - Class IV.
Patient must not have drug allergy to ingredient eribulin.
Patient must not be pregnant.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of HIV infection.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Viral hepatitis C.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Hepatitis B.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Transient ischemic attack.
Patient must not have adverse event of Grade 3 Peripheral sensory neuropathy.
Patient must not have adverse event of Grade 4 Peripheral sensory neuropathy.
Patient must not have adverse event of Grade 3 Peripheral motor neuropathy.
Patient must not have adverse event of Grade 4 Peripheral motor neuropathy.
Patient must not have adverse event of Grade 5 Peripheral motor neuropathy.
```
