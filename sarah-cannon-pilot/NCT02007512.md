Target Profile
```
Patient's age must be greater than or equal to 18 years.
Patient's gender must be female.
Patient must have active diagnosis of Human epidermal growth factor 2 negative carcinoma of breast.
Patient must have active diagnosis of Estrogen receptor positive tumor OR Progesterone receptor positive tumor.
Patient must have a health evaluation score of ECOG performance status grade:Prid:Pt:^Patient:Nom:Observed less than or equal to 1.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of Metastasis to brain.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Metastasis to brain.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Leptomeningeal sarcoma.
Patient must not have active diagnosis of HIV infection.
Patient must not have historical diagnosis of Seizure.
Patient must not have historical medication within drug class Androgen Receptor Inhibitor.
Patient must not have historical medication within drug class Estrogen within less than or equal to 14 days.
Patient must not have historical medication within drug class Androgen.
Patient must not have drug allergy to ingredient exemestane.
```
